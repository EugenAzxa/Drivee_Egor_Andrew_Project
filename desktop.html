Add Drivee to your iPhone home screen

  1. Open drivee.ca in Safari (not Chrome — Apple only allows install from Safari).
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen.

  4. Confirm the name (Drivee) and tap Add. The icon appears on your home screen — tap it like any app.
